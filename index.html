VALORANT

ABOUT

Valorant is a 5v5 character-based tactical shooter. With gunplay and special abilities, 13 rounds are needed to secure the victory through both attack and defense. Every player has one-life per round and they must be creative in order to get an upperhand on their opponents. Different agents have different skill sets which must be utilized properly to gain the most value out of them. Guns and abilities are purchased at the beginning of every round with the credits earned from each round win or loss. With different games modes to play on, players can select whether they want to earn ranks based on their performance in Competitive or play a casual game of Unranked or Spike Rush. Gunplay can be practiced in Deathmatch and all these modes are played in a pool of 6 maps.
